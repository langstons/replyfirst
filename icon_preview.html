Design Notes

  • Concept: Three horizontal bars represent email threads in a conversation. The upward arrow indicates that newest messages appear first (reversed order).
  • Colors:
    Inactive: #9CA3AF (Gray)
    Active: #8B5CF6 → #7C3AED (Purple gradient)
  • Visual Hierarchy: The bars are evenly spaced to represent email threads, with the arrow providing clear directional context.
  • Scalability: The design works at all sizes (16px-128px) with appropriate scaling adjustments for each size.
  • Accessibility: Works in both light and dark themes. The icon is recognizable even in monochrome.
  • Brand Alignment: Purple gradient matches the popup UI (#667eea → #764ba2 theme).
